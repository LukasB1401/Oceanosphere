OCEANOSPHERE

Clean Water Through Solar Innovation

The Challenge

According to the WHO and UNICEF, 2.2 billion people still lack access to clean water. Most of them are in Africa or the Middle East. It's time to act.

Our Solution

We developed a self-sufficient sphere that draws water from natural sources. Powered by solar energy, it purifies water through evaporation, leaving salt and bacteria behind. Clean water then flows to the bottom, ready for use — no external power needed.

Our Mission

We aim to make OCEANOSPHERE available to individual households in developed countries, enabling them to become water-independent. The profits from these sales will help fund humanitarian missions to install spheres in African regions that lack access to clean water.
